FAQs


Most Frequent Questions & Answers

We are here to help you succeed!

1. What tools do I need to bring?


You need to bring
- Text Books
- Stationery
- Laptops or Tablet
- We offer A4 papers and pens

2. How is the system?


You have to study by sessions.
1 session costs 5000/- Ks + free a cup of coffee or drinks

3. How can I book session?


You can book by calling 09-12345678.
You can book, cancel or reschedule your session.

4. How long is each session?


1 session - 2 hours
If you take 2 sessions (4hours), you will get 1 hour free as a gift.

5. What is trail session?


Trail session is for first time customer who wants to experience our study cafe.
You will get the 1 hour session with 2000/- Ks but you won't get a cup of free drink or coffee.

6. What happens if I miss my session?


If you miss your session, we will refund 75% of your session.

7. Can I get a refund for my leftover session credits?


To be honest, you can't get a refund for your leftover session credits.

8. Why should I choose Cheshire Study Cafe?


Our Cafe Shop has quit, clam, aesthetic enviroment for students and employee to do their works.
Has Multiple of plug socket for your laptops and tablets.
Has Strong Wifi Connection.

9. any RULES?


We have Rules for our study cafe.
1. Cell phone use is not allowed.
2. Private discussion is not allowed. 3. Avoid using two seats by sitting on one and placing your laggage, clothing, etc,.

10. Do you have Outdoor Seating?


Yes, We have Outdoor Seating for Private Discussion, Meeting Appointments.
